# One-Day Meal Plan for Marathon Preparation

Here is a one-day meal plan for an athlete preparing for marathon competition with carbohydrate loading and optimal hydration:

* **Calorie Target:** Approximately 3,200 calories
* **Macronutrient Breakdown:** Protein (120g), Fat (85g), Carbs (480g)

## Meal Plan:

* **Breakfast (7:00 AM):** Oatmeal (100g dry weight) with banana (150g), honey (25g), and whole milk (250g). 1 slice whole wheat toast (40g) with almond butter (20g).
  * Protein: 18g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 95g

* **Mid-Morning Snack (10:00 AM):** Energy bar (65g) and sports drink (500mL with 6% carbohydrate solution).
  * Protein: 8g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 65g

* **Lunch (1:00 PM):** Grilled chicken breast (140g), brown rice (120g dry weight), steamed carrots (150g), and olive oil dressing (15g). Orange juice (250mL).
  * Protein: 42g, Fat: 12g, Carbs: 110g

* **Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM):** Greek yogurt (200g, low-fat) with granola (40g) and mixed berries (100g).
  * Protein: 18g, Fat: 8g, Carbs: 60g

* **Pre-Run Snack (6:00 PM):** White bread sandwich (80g) with jam (30g) and banana (120g). Sports drink (300mL).
  * Protein: 8g, Fat: 4g, Carbs: 95g

* **Dinner (7:30 PM):** Lean ground turkey (150g), pasta (110g dry weight), tomato-based sauce (150g), and olive oil (10g). Side salad with vinaigrette (30g).
  * Protein: 45g, Fat: 18g, Carbs: 110g

* **Evening Snack (9:30 PM):** Honey (20g) dissolved in warm milk (200g).
  * Protein: 7g, Fat: 3g, Carbs: 25g

This meal plan prioritizes complex carbohydrates for glycogen storage, adequate protein for muscle recovery, and consistent hydration throughout the day to support marathon preparation and endurance performance.
